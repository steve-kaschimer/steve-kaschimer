## 💡 What I Do

I'm a Senior Consultant in the Cloud practice at Slalom US, based in Detroit, Michigan, where I work primarily across Azure‑centric cloud, application, and DevOps initiatives, with documented skills spanning Azure, AWS, GCP, .NET, solution and application architecture.

Day to day that means working alongside Slalom's customers to design and implement the technology, adjust processes and imprve skills that they need to solve their business problems.

A few things I spend most of my time on:

- 🏢 Designing and Implementing Solid DevSecOps — security-first, cost-aware, and maintainable by the team that inherits it
- ☁️ Designing and Implementing Cloud-Based Solutions - Azure is my chosen platform, .NET and React tend to be my goto stack
- 👨‍🏫 Sharing what works — and what didn't — internally via Teams and Slack Channels
